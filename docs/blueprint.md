# **App Name**: Eternità Onoranze

## Core Features:

- H24 Urgency Hub: A prominent, floating WhatsApp interaction point labeled 'Urgenze H24' for immediate, empathetic connection with grieving families.
- Compassion AI Tool: An intelligent widget to help users understand costs, procedures, and options through an AI tool providing sensitive first-line information.
- Firestore Lead Repository: Integration with Firestore to securely archive all inquiries from the contact form as a redundant backup to ensure no request is lost.
- Nodemailer Notification Handler: Automatic dispatch of email notifications via Nodemailer to agency staff immediately upon new inquiry submission.
- Local SEO Optimization: Region-specific configuration including automated sitemaps and robots.txt focused on the Terracina, Latina, and province areas.
- Modern Service Portfolio: A high-fidelity grid displaying core agency offerings such as cremation, transport, and administrative support in a dignified UI.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Midnight Blue (#0F172A), evoking stability and deep solemnity.
- Background Color: Ultra-soft Slate (#F8FAFC), chosen to provide a breathing, comfortable canvas that avoids clinical sterility.
- Accent Color: Warm Bronze (#B45309), applied to critical elements to add a layer of prestige and comforting warmth.
- Font pairing: 'Playfair' (Serif) for headlines to provide institutional dignity, and 'Inter' (Sans-serif) for body text to ensure maximum readability.
- Spacious, minimalist layout that prioritizes high white-space usage to evoke calm and respectful silence.
- Extremely slow transitions (fades) that mirror the pace of a quiet and respectful ceremony.
- Thin-lined, minimalist iconography representing support, hands, and architectural strength.
